ABOUT ONE PIECE

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew in search of the legendary One Piece treasure.

This website explores the rich world and characters created by Eiichiro Oda.
